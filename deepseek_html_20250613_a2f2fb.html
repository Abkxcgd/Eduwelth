Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our platform

What's the difference between active and passive income? +
Active income is earned for each direct referral you make. Passive income is earned when your referrals make referrals (second level). This creates a sustainable earning stream.
How do I get paid? +
Earnings are credited to your EduWealth wallet immediately. You can withdraw to your bank account or UPI once you reach ₹500. Withdrawals process within 24 hours.
What is the "Bonus Unlock" in Elite package? +
Elite members unlock our exclusive "Mentor Program" where you earn ₹500 for every student you personally coach to Silver tier or higher. This is in addition to regular referral earnings.
Is there any monthly fee? +
No! All packages are one-time payments with lifetime access. There are no recurring fees or hidden charges.
